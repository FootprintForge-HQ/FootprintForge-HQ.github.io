Service Agreements & FAQ

Operational Protocols
How does the zero monthly fee structure work?

We deploy your static digital framework on robust, open-source infrastructure (like GitHub Pages) which incurs zero monthly server costs. You pay a one-time deployment fee to Footprint Forge. If you choose to link a custom domain name (e.g., yourname.com), you only pay the standard annual renewal fee to your domain registrar (usually around ₹800/year). We do not charge you rent.

What is included in the Scope of Architecture?

Our service encompasses the deployment of static digital frameworks (based on your chosen tier) and the physical manufacturing of smart cards. Ground-up custom web development, dynamic backend databases, or ongoing content management fall outside our structural scope. Clients also receive PDF and PNG formats of their final card design for personal use.

How long does hardware manufacturing take?

Physical cards are manufactured using the client's selected tier. Premium PVC cards typically ship within 5-7 business days after your digital portfolio is approved. Due to the bespoke nature of the hardware, physical products cannot be refunded once manufacturing commences.

What are the payment terms?

A 50% non-refundable deposit is required to commence digital architecture and allocate hardware materials. The final 50% balance clears upon your approval of the live digital portfolio, prior to the dispatch of your physical assets.
