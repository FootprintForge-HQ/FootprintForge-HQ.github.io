Operational Protocols

Service Agreement

By proceeding with Footprint Forge services, you agree to the following streamlined protocols designed to ensure a precise, professional delivery:

1. Scope of Architecture

Our service encompasses the deployment of static digital frameworks and the physical forging of smart cards. Ground-up custom web development or dynamic backend functionalities fall outside our structural scope. Clients receive PDF and PNG formats of their final card design for personal use.

2. Infrastructure & Routing

GitHub Pages hosting incurs zero monthly fees. If the Client requires a custom top-level domain, the Client assumes responsibility for purchasing and renewing that domain. Footprint Forge will provide standard DNS routing assistance.

3. Hardware Forging

Physical cards are manufactured using the client's selected tier. Wood and metal upgrades feature a precision laser-engraved monogram. Due to the bespoke nature of the hardware, physical products cannot be refunded once forging commences.

4. Capital Transfer

A 50% non-refundable deposit is required to commence digital architecture and hardware allocation. The final 50% balance clears upon approval of the digital portfolio, prior to the dispatch of physical assets.
