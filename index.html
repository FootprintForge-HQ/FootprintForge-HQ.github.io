Frequently Asked Questions

How It Works
How does the zero monthly fee work?

We build your website using free, secure platforms (like GitHub Pages) so there are no monthly hosting bills. You pay us once to build it. If you want a custom ".com" name, you just pay the standard yearly domain fee (around ₹800/year). We do not charge you rent.

What exactly am I buying?

You get a fully built digital portfolio based on the design you choose, plus a physical smart card. (Note: We build static portfolio sites, not complex web apps with login portals or databases.) You also get PDF and PNG copies of your card design.

How long does it take to get my card?

Once you review and approve the final design of your digital portfolio, your physical smart card is printed and shipped. This usually takes 5-7 business days.

How does payment work?

You pay a 50% deposit to get started. The final 50% is only paid after you review and approve your live digital portfolio, right before we ship your physical card.
